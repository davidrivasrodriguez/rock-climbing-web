Sarah Chen

The Spider

Canadian 25 years old

Starting at age 12, Sarah quickly rose through the ranks of competitive climbing. Known for her incredible finger strength and dynamic style, she's revolutionized several classic boulder problems with her innovative beta.

  • World Cup Gold Medalist 2024
  • National Record Holder - Speed Climbing
